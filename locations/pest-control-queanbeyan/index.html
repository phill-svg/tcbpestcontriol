[FAQ- 01 ]

Are you licensed to work on the NSW side of the border?

Yes — we hold both NSW EPA and ACT pesticide licensing, so the same technician and treatment standard applies whether you're in Queanbeyan, Jerrabomberra or across in the ACT.

[FAQ- 02 ]

Do newer estates like Googong get the same termite risk as older Queanbeyan streets?

Risk is lower but not zero — newer Googong builds are more exposed to post-construction landscaping issues, while the older residential pockets closer to central Queanbeyan see more standard timber-frame termite activity.

Is pricing different because Queanbeyan is technically NSW?

No — we quote the same way regardless of which side of the border you're on: a written, upfront quote before any work starts.
