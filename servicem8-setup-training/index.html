[01] Why ServiceM8

What it actually changes.

ServiceM8 is job management built for trade and service businesses — quoting, scheduling, invoicing and customer communication in one place, run from your phone between jobs.

Scheduling & dispatch

See every job and every technician on one calendar, and move things around without a phone call.

Quoting & invoicing on-site

Quote, get sign-off, and invoice from the job — no driving back to the office to do paperwork.

Automated customer updates

Booking confirmations, on-my-way texts and follow-ups go out without you touching a keyboard.

Job & staff tracking

Know where your team is and what's been done, without chasing anyone for an update.

Real business numbers

Reporting that actually tells you what's working — job types, revenue, staff performance.

Runs from your phone

Built for the field — not a desktop system you have to remember to update later.
