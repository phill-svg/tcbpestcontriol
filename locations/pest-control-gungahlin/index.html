[05] FAQ · Gungahlin

Questions Gungahlin residents ask first.

[FAQ- 01 ]

My house was only built a few years ago — can it still get termites?

Yes. Post-construction landscaping and retained builder's timber are common termite entry points on newer Gungahlin builds. We check sub-floor vents, garden edging and any timber left in contact with soil during the first inspection.

[FAQ- 02 ]

Why do I keep finding ants on the kitchen bench in a new build?

Coastal brown and Argentine ants follow fresh garden beds and irrigation lines that come with new landscaping. A perimeter treatment around the foundation usually stops the trail before it reaches the kitchen.

[FAQ- 03 ]

Do you treat apartment and townhouse complexes in Harrison and Crace?

Yes — we run scheduled treatments for German cockroach pressure in townhouse strips and apartment complexes, with written reports supplied for body-corporate records.
