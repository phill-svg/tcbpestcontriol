[01] The principles

How we run every job.

One local family, one team

TCB is family-run and locally owned — not a franchise, not a call centre. The technician who quotes turns up to do the work, every time.

Fully licensed, fully insured

Every technician on the team holds an ACT and NSW pesticides licence, and the business carries full public liability insurance. Termite work is Termidor-accredited.

Family-safe treatment

Pet and child-friendly products by default. The quote spells out exactly what's being used, where, and the re-entry time in plain English — no acronyms, no jargon.

Same-week availability

Most quote requests get a written answer the same business day, and most treatments can be booked within a week. Urgent call-outs are prioritised.

Why a written report matters

Every visit closes with a typed report.

Useful for tenants, real estate agents, body corporates, auditors — and for your own peace of mind. The report names the species spotted, the products used, the locations treated and what to watch for next.
